Our Story

Where Fire
Meets Craft

Primitivo is not just a steakhouse — it's an experience carved in flame. In the heart of St. Julian's, we honour the primal art of fire-cooked cuisine. Premium Japanese Wagyu, hand-selected dry-aged cuts, and an open kitchen where every sear, every flame, every plate tells a story.

Our philosophy is simple: bold flavours, zero pretension. Every dish is crafted with obsessive attention, from the first char to the final garnish. This is meat, elevated.
